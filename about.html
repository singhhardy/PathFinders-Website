About Us

Path Builders Education Services is a trusted educational consultancy dedicated to helping students navigate their academic journeys after 12th for higher studies. From career counseling to admission guidance and study abroad opportunities, we provide end-to-end support tailored to each student’s goals.

Vision

To be a leading education consultancy known for integrity, innovation, and impact in shaping student futures. let me share a personal story related to higher education after 12th. Like many of you, I too faced the challenge of selecting the right path. After completing my high school education, I was filled with dreams, ambitions, and questions about my future. The pressure to make the right choices was immense, and the options seemed endless. It was a time of uncertainty and confusion.

Through the guidance of mentors and counselors, I eventually found my way to the perfect college, and I can honestly say it was a transformative experience. This journey not only helped me discover my true passion but also instilled in me a desire to help others navigate this crucial phase of life. That's why I founded this consultancy to ensure that no student feels lost or overwhelmed when it comes to higher education decisions.

Mission

Is to empower students like you to make informed decisions and secure admissions to the best-fit colleges and universities. With our team of experienced advisors and an extensive network of educational institutions, we are here to assist you every step of the way.
